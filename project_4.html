Overall Aim
For the merchant:

To improve conversion rate of the customers by creating seamless journeys by predicting customer intent, understanding customer behaviour using cross-merchant platform, user demographics and product details and attributes.

For the shopper:

Provide a seamless shopping experience through discovery by providing the right product recommendations at the right stage, targeted mail campaign and newsletters to get the customer back to website.

First impressions of the service is based on the observations before signing-up for the service. Since it is a paid service, the main website is how the potential customers/users know and evaluate before they start using it and decide on the service or one of its competitors instead. To circumvent this, they provide a free trial for 15 days where a vendor can use the system and evaluate the suitability and whether they would like it for the business.
